Hostel Hub

Welcome to the Hostel Complaint Management and Feedback System, a web-based platform dedicated to improving the living experience for students residing in hostels. This system empowers students to voice concerns and feedback, fostering a collaborative environment between students and hostel management.

Key Features

Complaint Submission

The system offers a user-friendly interface that allows students to submit complaints effortlessly.

Issue Categorization

Complaints can be categorized based on their nature, such as maintenance, noise, or food quality, for efficient organization.

Real-Time Tracking

Students can track the status of their complaints in real-time, providing transparency and updates.

Secure Communication

The system ensures secure communication channels for students to express their concerns and feedback confidentially.
